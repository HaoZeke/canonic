# Local facilities field is data collection only

Hello,

The form field that asks why you are not using a local or tier-2 facility is treated as **data collection** for the wider ecosystem discussion. For small infra requests, advisors **do not reject** an application solely on the basis of that free-text answer.

You may leave a short honest answer (for example that the local system lacks the required memory, software, GPU type, or free capacity). If the field is empty or unhelpful, it can be ignored for the technical decision unless there is a specific reason to follow up.

Please ensure the rest of the application still has a clear resource calculation and storage plan. Those remain the primary review points.

Regards,
Support Team
