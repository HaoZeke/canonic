# Permission to reproduce an issue in your account

Hello,

To reproduce this issue we may need to work in **your account context** (environment modules, project space paths, allocation, and file permissions). Advisor accounts have broad capacity for testing, but the preferred pattern is to use your context after you agree.

Please reply explicitly if you **grant permission** for an advisor to access your account for troubleshooting this ticket. You may limit the permission to specific paths or a time window if you prefer.

We will not change production data without discussing the plan with you first, except for read-only inspection needed to diagnose the problem.

Regards,
Support Team
