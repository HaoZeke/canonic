# Password reset self-service

Hello,

To reset your account password, use the self-service portal:

1. Open **https://id.example.com/reset**
2. Enter the email address on file
3. Follow the link in the verification message within **15 minutes**

If you do not receive the email, check spam and confirm the address matches your directory entry.

Do **not** share temporary passwords over chat. Escalate only if the portal returns `ACCOUNT_LOCKED` after three failed attempts.

Regards,
Support
